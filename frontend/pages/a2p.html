What is A2P 10DLC?

A2P 10DLC (Application-to-Person 10-Digit Long Code) is a US carrier standard that enables businesses to send SMS messages reliably and compliantly. Upgrading ensures your messages are delivered, protects against spam filtering, and maintains trust with mobile carriers. Without this upgrade, SMS features remain limited and may be filtered.
