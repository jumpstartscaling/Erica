Text Messaging Upgrade

Current status: Email only

The following capabilities are prepared but disabled:

  • LOCKED Webinar reminder texts
  • LOCKED Appointment reminder texts
  • LOCKED Two-way customer messaging
  • LOCKED Missed-webinar follow-up
  • LOCKED Reschedule notifications
  • LOCKED STOP and HELP automation

Why these features are unavailable

Application-to-person (A2P) text messaging to U.S. recipients over a standard 10-digit number requires business and campaign registration (10DLC), documented consent language, opt-out handling, and carrier approval. Until that registration is complete, the system intentionally remains email-only.
